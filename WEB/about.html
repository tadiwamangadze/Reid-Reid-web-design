About Us, Reid & Reid Contractors, LLLP

The Reid family has been in the grading and contracting industry in North Georgia since 1984. As a family owned and operated business, Reid & Reid Contractors, LLLP, understands the value of quality work, commitment to customers and excellent customer service.

Reid & Reid Contractors, LLLP can provide the knowledge and experience necessary to meet all your needs, whether that be grading, pipe installation, demolition or turn key font-sizete work.

In the last 20 years, Reid & Reid Contractors, LLLP has completed projects ranging from $100,000 to $12,000,000. We have a reputation of providing high quality results at a competitive price. Our employees take pride in exceeding the expectations of our clients and building relationships that last.

We offer design/build services for projects that include heavy highway, residential and commercial development for private developers, county agencies and the Georgia Department of Transportation.

Our success has afforded us the opportunity to develop strong relationships with stable subcontractors and vendors, as well as municipalities.
